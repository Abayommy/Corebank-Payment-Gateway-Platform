## 🎭 Presentation Delivery Guidelines

### **Executive Presentation Best Practices**

#### **Before the Presentation**
- **Audience Analysis**: Understand decision-makers, influencers, and concerns
- **Customization**: Tailor metrics and examples to specific audience
- **Rehearsal**: Practice timing, transitions, and Q&A responses
- **Materials**: Prepare executive summary, detailed appendix, demo assets

#### **During the Presentation**
- **Opening Impact**: Lead with most compelling business outcome
- **Data-Driven**: Use specific metrics and concrete examples
- **Visual Clarity**: Simple, professional slides with clear takeaways
- **Engagement**: Encourage questions and interactive discussion

#### **Follow-Up Actions**
- **Executive Summary**: Email key points and next steps within 24 hours
- **Detailed Proposal**: Provide comprehensive documentation
- **Timeline**: Establish clear decision milestones and deadlines
- **Relationship**: Maintain regular communication and updates
